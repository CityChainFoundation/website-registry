Register

So you want to register your own entity? Then this is the right place.

All entities registrated in the City Chain Registry is subfixed with the abbrevation CC, which is short for Crypto Company.

If your entity name is "Joe's Bar", then the full entity registration will be "Joe's Bar CC".

Registration Process

The registration process works as follows:

First you create an identity for your entity using City Hub. This will store the identity that represents the entity. You will need to use City Hub also for the final step, which is signing the entity registration.

After you have your identity published, you will be able to look it up and prefill some of the fields below.
